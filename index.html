What Is GEO?

If You're Not in
AI Search, You
Don't Exist.

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of positioning your firm to be cited and recommended when AI tools answer legal questions. It's different from traditional SEO — ranking #1 on Google doesn't mean you appear in ChatGPT. They are separate games.

80% of pages cited in AI answers don't even rank in Google's top 100. That means your current SEO investment provides zero protection in AI search. And as AI search grows — tripling in query volume in under a year — that gap gets more expensive every month you wait.

The firms that build AI search presence now will be nearly impossible to displace. The ones that wait will be playing catch-up against competitors who are already being recommended by name.

How We Do It

Four Ways We Get
Your Firm Into
AI Search Results.

GEO isn't one tactic. It's a set of interconnected signals that tell AI tools your firm is authoritative, trustworthy, and relevant to legal questions in your market.

01

Authority Content AI Tools Actually Cite

AI search tools pull answers from structured, authoritative content. We build the practice area pages, FAQs, and legal guides that get cited as the answer when potential clients ask AI about attorneys in your market. If the content doesn't exist, AI has nothing to recommend.

02

Review Velocity & Reputation Signals

AI search heavily weights Google Reviews, rating patterns, recency, and sentiment. A firm with 50 reviews from 2021 looks different to AI than one with 200 reviews from 2025. We build the systematic review generation process that keeps your reputation signals fresh and strong.

03

Entity Optimization & Structured Data

We configure your firm's complete digital footprint — schema markup, directory consistency across 50+ platforms, knowledge graph presence, and attorney entity profiles — so AI systems correctly understand who you are, what you practice, and where you serve clients.

04

Ongoing AI Search Monitoring

We actively track when and how your firm appears across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overview — identifying gaps and opportunities before competitors catch on. AI search results are fluid; what works today needs maintenance tomorrow.
